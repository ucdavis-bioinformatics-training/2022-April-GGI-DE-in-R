## Files to download

Cut and paste these lines of code into your RStudio console to download the necessary analysis files to your R working directory:

```
download.file("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ucdavis-bioinformatics-training/2022-April-GGI-DE-in-R/master/data_analysis/DE_Analysis.Rmd", file.path(getwd(), "DE_Analysis.Rmd"))
download.file("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ucdavis-bioinformatics-training/2022-April-GGI-DE-in-R/master/data_analysis/enrichment.Rmd", file.path(getwd(), "enrichment.Rmd"))
download.file("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ucdavis-bioinformatics-training/2022-April-GGI-DE-in-R/master/data_analysis/counts_for_course.txt", file.path(getwd(), "counts_for_course.txt"))
download.file("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ucdavis-bioinformatics-training/2022-April-GGI-DE-in-R/master/data_analysis/metadata_for_course.csv", file.path(getwd(), "metadata_for_course.csv"))
download.file("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ucdavis-bioinformatics-training/2022-April-GGI-DE-in-R/master/data_analysis/annotation.txt", file.path(getwd(), "annotation.txt"))
```
